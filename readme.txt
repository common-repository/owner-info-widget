This plugin enables the ability to display website brand/owner information on a widget.

== Description ==

This plugin enables the ability to add/display website brand/owner information on a widget. Also it includes all major social media platform's icons with their respective links.

It gives a prominent visual of your brand or your information in sidebar. It helps in building branding on your website, also provide your social proof. 

Here are few features of it:

*   Show your personal/brand photo.
*   Display short description.
*   Include social pages links.

== Installation ==

1. Upload the plugin zip file to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory, or install meBounce through the WordPress plugins screen directly.
2. Activate the Owner Info Widget through the 'Plugins' screen in WordPress
3. Navigate to widget menu from appearance menu add Owner Info Widget to your sidebar and fill up the form with your details.
